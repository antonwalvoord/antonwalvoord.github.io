Keepsake

2026-03-01

What is there to say about possessions?
What do they say about your heart?

Each treasure is a decision, carved by
a lifetime etching its torrent
into the shape of you

The chipping patina on a camera,
grips wellworn by the oils and prints
of cherished hands

Those years, the physicality imbuing a soul.
It speaks.

The tools of the heart
lay waiting for remembering
