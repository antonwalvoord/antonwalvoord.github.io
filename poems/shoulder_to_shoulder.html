Shoulder to Shoulder

2026-03-15

I will sit shoulder to shoulder with you on the train
We will jostle on the tracks and
feel the heart squeezing of
accidental glancing

And I will know that my choice was right
and my spirit will be filled, and yours
and we will look out at the electrical
poles and streetlights and slush and
know the quiet peace

And our kitchen will be laughter and
gentle brewing at dawn and music
of our uninhibited voices

And I will know you
