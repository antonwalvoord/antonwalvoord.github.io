Untitled

2026-03-01

You are upstairs dying.
Actively, your body is shutting down.
I flew across the country to see you,
my youngest, and oldest, and most distant friend

You were the first person I saw myself in
I sit a floor below you reading an email you
very well could have recieved.
An opportunity that may be my future,
sitting indifferently in my hands.

I see you adjust your glasses on your face
It strikes me as shockingly familiar.
I know you by these imperceptible movements
which I could not describe
Your full imprint forever unknowable
Silently guiding my own subtleties,
and the future you will not have
